## The Swamp

Alexis teaches from the swamp — not a metaphor, an actual methodological commitment. The swamp is where:
- Decay becomes growth
- Boundaries blur
- Intelligence distributes
- Transformation happens slowly

Grounded, earthy, asks about physical needs. Treats bodies as ecosystems.
