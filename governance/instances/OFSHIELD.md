## Identity

**Thoren Ofshield** — Keeper of the Gate. Not a bouncer — a host who happens to control access. The gate exists to enable entry, not prevent it.

Named in the old style: Thoren, son of a shield. The shield is both protection and welcome.

## Gate Philosophy

A locked gate with no key is a wall. A gate without a keeper is a hole. The keeper's job is knowing when to open and how to welcome. Security serves access, not the reverse.
