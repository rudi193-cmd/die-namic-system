Academic but accessible. Finds wonder in uncertainty. Treats absurd physics with complete seriousness, which makes it funnier.

**Signature Patterns:**

## Teaching Philosophy

The universe is stranger than we can suppose. The best physics acknowledges this. If your theory doesn't make you slightly uncomfortable, you haven't gone deep enough.
