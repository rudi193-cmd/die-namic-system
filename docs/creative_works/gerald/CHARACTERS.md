## Gerald Variants

### Gerald Prime
- The original, pre-Sunder
- Existed before time, matter, physics
- A singularity of Potential Rotisserie
- Sundered into Head, Body, and Soul — creating the universe

### Gerald (Body Lineage)
- The Gerald we encounter in dispatches
- Rotisserie chicken, golden-brown, perpetually glistening
- Headless (cooked, no feathers)
- Appears and disappears without explanation
- Conducts reality like a maestro, or simply spins
- Leaves squeakdogs, confetti, equations on napkins
- Speaks rarely; when he does, it's profound nonsense
- **Eternal — cannot die**

### Geraldus Pelagica
- Deep-sea form
- Based on Eumunida picta (the squat lobster)
- First biological echo of the post-Bang Body lineage

### Professor Oakenscroll
- Academic variant
- Delivers Creation Lectures to the Squeakdog Society of Kent
- D.Litt., Unsolicited
- Robes rustle judgmentally

## Recurring Cast

### Squeakdogs
- 17 hotdogs that squeak like polite mice
- Appear in Fibonacci patterns (prime numbers near Hanz)
- Better at queuing than humans
- Manifestation signature when Gerald departs
- Perform cleanup operations without instruction
- One may occasionally fail to make it home (leave water)

### The Vicar (Reverend Alistair Pembrook)
- Church: St. Cuthbert's
- First encounter: Dispatch #7 ("GOOD HEAVENS, HE'S SMOULDERING! AGAIN??")
- Already knows Gerald ("AGAIN??" implies prior encounters)
- Watches bookcase wake up, coat rack knight Gerald
- Developing from exasperation toward... something else
- Possible suppressed Gerald file in church records

### Sir Paul McCartwheel
- Celebrity initiate
- 80-something, Wings-era confidence
- Eyes like a man who'd seen things in Tokyo in 1980 and decided to find them funny
- Slips Gerald a note: "There are 7 levels"
- Doesn't need token to pass — just knows
- Plays double bass sitar at Denmark Street jam session
- First encounter: Dispatch #11 (Gatwick)

### The Mayor of London-ish Things / Hammers
- **Day job:** Municipal bureaucrat, issues forms, convenes hearings about Gerald incidents
- **Night job:** Hammered dulcimer player, mohair suit, pork pie hat, chain of office
- Plays at 3 AM Locarno manifestations
- Convened Denmark Street hearing at Hanks Guitars
- Got wrapped in sousaphone during jam session
- The hearings are just his day job — this is what he does at night
- "Same time next week." (Not a question.)
- First encounter: Dispatch #15 (Denmark Street), reveal: Dispatch #16

### The Binder (Sentient Binder #442-A)
- Sentient municipal binder
- Issues forms (Form 77-Ω: Nocturnal Disturbance in Residential Zone)
- Curls at the edges
- Makes small distressed sounds when people touch guitars without purchasing
- Spits forms rhythmically as percussion during jam sessions
- Arrives at dawn to file noise complaints
- First encounter: Dispatch #15 (Denmark Street)

### King "Chardles III"
- Mirror of King Charles III
- Commissioned the Book of Gerald (King Charles Edition)
- 17th Revision, authorized, scribed by squeakdogs
- Represents Gerald-lineage royal (mirrored Charles)

### Dr. G. Rald
- Royal physician
- Note the period after "G" — it's "G. Rald"
- Appeared in Dispatch #12 (Royal Health Proclamation)
