## Overview

Gerald is an enlightened rotisserie chicken who exists as a semi-omnipresent trickster entity. He achieved enlightenment through accidental centrifugal chakra alignment over seven presidential administrations while rotating on a grocery store rotisserie.

This is a British absurdist comedy series in the tradition of Douglas Adams and Terry Pratchett, published primarily to r/DispatchesFromReality.

## The Heart of Gerald

Gerald exists because the universe is absurd, but absurdity can be warm.

He's not satire. He's not parody. He's not commentary.

He's what happens when someone asks: "What if enlightenment was accidental, physics was delicious, and chaos queued politely?"

The answer is always chicken.
