## The Complete Stone

**Assembly:** Three stone fragments combine in the Locket of Warning

**First Function:** Each fragment frees one trapped soul
- Seed of Life → wife
- Stone of Order → Beatrice  
- Stone of Balance → Abigail

**Second Function:** When combined in the Locket, generates a 30-foot cone shield

**Purpose:** The shield protects the party during the 10-minute escape through the collapsing mine
