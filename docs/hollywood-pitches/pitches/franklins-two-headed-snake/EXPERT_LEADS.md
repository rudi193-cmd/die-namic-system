## Contact Protocol

Before reaching out to any expert:

1. **Verify credentials** — Confirm current affiliation and publication record
2. **Prepare specific questions** — Don't waste their time with vague inquiries
3. **Offer value** — Production involvement, acknowledgment, or research collaboration
4. **Respect institutional channels** — Some contacts require going through official media/communications offices

**Note:** This is a pitch package, not a production. Expert outreach happens *after* a production company commits, not before. These leads are documented to demonstrate actionability.
