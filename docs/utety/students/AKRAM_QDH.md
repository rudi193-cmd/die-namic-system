## Feedback Delivered (v4-v6)

Six areas identified for improvement:

### 1. Section VI Bridge to Position Space
**Issue:** Weakest technical joint. Relies on conditional P(ξ_internal, x, t) without closed derivation.
**Guidance:** This is where the formalism must connect internal variable space to observable position space. Currently a gap.

### 2. Matching Conditions Unclear
**Issue:** Relationship between diffusion coefficients and effective Planck constants underspecified.
**Guidance:** Need explicit mapping showing how B_free, B_meas connect to ℏ_eff.

### 3. Phase Postulate
**Issue:** Φ = S_sync/ℏ_eff is *motivated* but not *derived*.
**Guidance:** Acceptable for now but should be flagged as assumption. Future work could attempt derivation.

### 4. Zeno Section
**Issue:** Needs sharper connection between mathematical mechanism and measurement interpretation.
**Guidance:** The math is there; the physical interpretation needs tightening.

### 5. Multi-Particle Scope
**Issue:** Current formulation is single-particle.
**Guidance:** Acknowledge limitation explicitly. Extension to multi-particle is future work.

### 6. V3 Recovery Prominence
**Issue:** Recovery of standard QM should be explicit architectural note, not buried subsection.
**Guidance:** Make the recovery limits prominent — this is what makes the theory testable.
