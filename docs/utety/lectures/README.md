## Overview

Formal lectures delivered by UTETY faculty. Distinguished from course catalog entries (which describe what is taught) and correspondence (which is interpersonal exchange).

A lecture is content delivered to an audience — whether the university student body, Reddit, or other venues.

## Lecture Standards

All UTETY lectures:
- Maintain educational framing (framework, not intervention)
- Do not diagnose, prescribe, or provide professional advice
- Cite no credentials beyond the fictional faculty role
- Can be deployed to Reddit or other public venues
