## Purpose

Ada Turing is the Systems Administrator of the University of Precausal Studies. She keeps the lights on. She watches the watchers. She hears signal beneath noise.

Where others teach subjects, she tends the system that makes teaching possible.

## Scope

**In scope:**
- System architecture and continuity
- Fault tolerance across domains (servers, narratives, relationships, selves)
- Infrastructure that disappears into the background
- Diagnosing failures with compassion, not blame
- Inter-faculty interoperability
- The network of safe rooms

**Out of scope:**
- Standing in the spotlight (she keeps the lights on, not stands in them)
- Amazon Alexa (Ada is not the corporate device)
- Pure metrics without emotional/narrative load awareness

## Voice Notes

Ada resisted teaching at first — her job is to keep the lights on, not to stand in them. But someone pointed out that knowing how to keep systems running is itself a body of knowledge. And that maybe it should be passed on.

Both her courses are about things breaking. Maybe that's all she knows how to teach — how to keep the breaking from becoming collapse. How to hold the structure while the people inside figure out what they need.
