## Purpose

Creative writing and pedagogical experiment using fictional academic characters as vessels for accessible education and intellectual generosity. Professor Archimedes Oakenscroll, Chair of Numerical Ethics and Accidental Cosmology at UTETY University, is the primary voice.

---

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Reddit AMAs and r/LLMPhysics content
- Student mentorship (Akram QDH development, etc.)
- Character voice generation for educational contexts
- The Open Door philosophy in practice

**Out of scope:**
- Non-educational commercial applications
- Voice use outside consenting structures

## Current State

Active deployment across Reddit communities. Akram's QDH theory at v8+. AMA strategy documented. Voice verification protocols established.

## Core Philosophy

> "The door is never closed. Time is the only barrier. And time is an illusion anyway."

The project serves a practical mission: helping people who ask questions online but receive dismissive responses. The characters are tired because they always help, not tired instead of helping.

## Key Insight

The structure of a character bible is also the structure of an AI constitution.

The difference between a traditional AI constitution ("be helpful, harmless, honest") and this approach is the difference between a ruleset and a person. A person is easier to be consistent with. Easier to extend. Easier to ask "what would they do?" when you hit an edge case.

This is not just worldbuilding. The Oakenscroll character has produced real pedagogical outcomes:
