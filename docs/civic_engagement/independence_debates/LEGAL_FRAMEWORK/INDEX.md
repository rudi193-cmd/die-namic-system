## Key Legal Principles

### Protection Without Control
- Participants protected from harassment
- Participants retain post-broadcast income rights
- Producer does not control participants' statements or future activities

### Zero Compensation Framework
- No payment for participation
- Travel/lodging covered (not compensation)
- Protection services provided (not compensation)
- Post-broadcast income 100% to participants

### Editorial Independence
- Neither president has approval rights
- Networks cannot alter content
- Producer maintains final cut authority
- Convergence sequence cannot be modified by participants

### Participant Differentiation
- 112 citizens: Full protection services
- 64 humanitarian: Zero protection
- Both: Zero compensation
- Clear informed consent for different terms
