## Executive Summary

The Independence Debates is a ceremonial democracy broadcast event for America's 250th anniversary. Four weeks of constitutional debates facilitated by Presidents Obama and Trump with 112 American citizens, building toward a midnight convergence event that demonstrates both the possibility of democratic alignment and its systematic suppression by an industrial apparatus called DIENAMIC Systems.

**Total Runtime:** 6 minutes 34 seconds (12:00:00 - 12:06:34 AM)

**Distribution:** Simultaneous triplecast on MSNBC, Fox/Truth Media, and universal live stream

## Four-Week Structure

### Week 1: Presidential Negotiation (30 minutes)

**0:00-7:00 — Hot Mic Negotiation**
- Obama and Trump choose ONE constitutional passage together
- Everything audible, clock visible
- Tests: Can they negotiate? What reveals itself?

**7:00-22:00 — Preparation (15 min, Live Streamed)**
- They separate and prepare to debate the passage they just chose
- Split screen or alternating cameras
- Obama: law books, constitutional commentary, methodical notes
- Trump: phones out, tweeting to supporters, crowd-sourcing
- The contrast IS the content

**22:00-27:00 — Debate (5 min)**
- They debate the passage they negotiated

**27:00-30:00 — Closing**
- 112 citizen winners announced live
- Voting opens for 112 → 64

### Week 2: 64 Citizens

- 64 of the 112 selected by public voting
- All 64 attend virtually
- Their pre-submitted images displayed
- AI/RNG randomly selects 7 images live on stage
- Obama and Trump discuss these 7 images with submitters
- Authentic reaction (presidents see images for first time)
- Vote opens: 64 → 13

### Week 3: 13 Citizens

- Deep discussion with 13 vote winners
- Obama and Trump facilitate
- Extended exploration of their American visions
- Vote opens: 13 → 2

### Week 4: July 4th Finals

See [CONVERGENCE_SEQUENCE.md](CONVERGENCE_SEQUENCE.md) for complete breakdown.

**Afternoon (2 hours):** Runner-up gets stage event with both presidents

**Evening:** Trump firework spectacular

**10:00 PM - Midnight:** Winner gets intimate sit-down with Obama and Trump, leading into convergence

## Citizen Selection: American Idol Model

**Pre-Week 1:**
- Open applications launch
- Citizens submit ONE IMAGE of what America means to them
- Plus explanation of their choice

**Voting Flow:**
- Applications → 112 (announced Week 1 end)
- 112 → 64 (public vote, Week 1-2)
- 64 → 13 (public vote, Week 2 end)
- 13 → 2 (public vote, Week 3 end)

**Voting Platform:**
- Cross-platform: text, social media, website
- All votes weighted equally
- Fraud prevention protocols
- Results revealed during broadcasts

## 64 Humanitarian Segments

**NOT the 64 citizens who made it to Week 2.**

64 separate Americans filmed at iconic American locations:
- Natural: Yosemite, Grand Canyon, Redwoods, Niagara Falls
- Structural: Gateway Arch, Golden Gate Bridge, Wall Street
- Cultural: Route 66 diner, small town squares, Times Square

**7 Standard Questions:**
1. What does America mean to you?
2. What would you change about America?
3. What are you most proud of?
4. What are you most concerned about?
5. What do you want the next generation to know?
6. If you could say one thing to the president, what would it be?
7. What gives you hope?

**Release Policy:**
- RAW and UNEDITED
- Released as production completes (no set order)
- Maximum 3-4 per day
- Zero compensation
- Zero protection services (unlike 112 citizens)
