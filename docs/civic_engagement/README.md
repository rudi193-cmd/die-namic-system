## Overview

Public-facing civic engagement projects that share philosophical DNA with the Die-namic System: citizen empowerment, spectacle + substance, democratic participation grounded in shared artifacts.

These are external concepts, not system components. Released under Creative Commons for anyone to use, adapt, and implement.

## Projects

### [Independence Debates](independence_debates/)

**The Independence Debates: A Constitutional Conversation**

Presidential-scale debate format for America's 250th anniversary (July 4, 2026). Obama + Trump + 112 citizens across 4 weeks, culminating in midnight Oval Office convergence.

- [CONCEPT_v1.0.md](independence_debates/CONCEPT_v1.0.md) — Full framework specification
- [PRODUCTION_PLAN.md](independence_debates/PRODUCTION_PLAN.md) — Budget, distribution, broadcast timeline
- [OUTREACH_LOG.md](independence_debates/OUTREACH_LOG.md) — Contact status tracking

### [Albuquerque Debates](albuquerque_debates/)

**Albuquerque Mayoral Debate Framework**

Local debate format using historical photographs from *Early Albuquerque: A Photographic History, 1870-1918* as prompts instead of moderator questions.

- [FRAMEWORK_v1.0.md](albuquerque_debates/FRAMEWORK_v1.0.md) — Complete local format specification
- [OUTREACH_LOG.md](albuquerque_debates/OUTREACH_LOG.md) — KOB TV pitch status

---

## Philosophy

> "It's harder to bullshit a photograph than a moderator's question."

Both projects replace traditional debate mechanics (gotcha questions, soundbites) with shared artifacts that force genuine engagement: Constitutional passages, historical photographs, citizen stories.
